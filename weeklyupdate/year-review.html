December 14, 2020

2020 Summary

   
 
 
 
 
 

Hi team,

With the Holiday season approaching we've taken a step back to reflect on the past 12 months. Here's what came out of it: a timeline summary of your 2020 with TrueNorth:
 

Q1

8 Prod releases

We started the year by rolling out several fixes and features, including the Bank Reconciliation report

  
 

Q2

9 Prod releases

Along with more fixes and smaller improvements, new features were added, like tipping management users, the ability to set a custom overdraft limit per restaurant, and improved internal slack notifications for production monitoring

Apr 30

We ran a penetration test and fixed the vulnerabilities found

  
 

Jun 25

We made changes to our Scheduler service that decreased processing times of the Ingo settlement files by more than 90%

Mid-Year

Enter the Kickfin tech team

Around the mid year mark we started working alongside the newly formed Kickfin tech team, collaborating on a daily basis. From this point onwards we worked on rolling out bigger functionalities for v1 and, in parallel, work on the new v2 platform micro services (Payout, Developer API, Heartland POS)

 
 

Q3

3 Prod releases

During this quarter we released friendlier customer facing error messages for failed payments and we added two new sections to the admin portal: Payments Overview and Payment History

Sep 28

The first v2 microservice was rolled out, the Payout service, which now handles payment requests made to Ingo and is compatible with the new Ingo PayAPI v11

 
 

Q4

6 Prod releases

In the past months more and more effort was put upon creating the v2 micro services. Meanwhile, we continue to add value to v1 with a new Canceled payments section in the admin portal and improved reminders for customers to alert about failed payments and card problems

Nov 19

On this day the Tip Holdbacks functionality is released and immediately adopted and used by restaurants

  
 

We've enjoyed contributing to your success throughout 2020. Here 's to an even more successful 2021!
